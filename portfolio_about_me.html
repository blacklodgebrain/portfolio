About Me

Hi there! My name is Isabella Pelot, I am an aspiring graphic designer. This summer, my focus is on working and saving for the upcoming college semester while also aiming to secure an internship. I'm dedicated to building a strong foundation for my academic journey and gaining valuable real-world experience to support my career goals.

At the age of 11, my fascination with art sparked my journey into graphic design. Drawing inspiration from my love for creativity and expression, I embarked on a path to explore the world of design.

In 2021, I made a significant move from Atlanta, Georgia, to North Idaho. This transition marked my entrance into public school after being homeschooled for most of my life. It was during this time that I discovered North Idaho College's graphic design program, thanks to the guidance of my counselor.

Currently, I'm enrolled in North Idaho College's graphic design program, where I'm eager to expand my knowledge and skills in the field. To me, design is more than just a profession—it's a creative journey that I'm excited to explore further.
